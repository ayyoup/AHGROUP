About Us

Alfa Holding Group was Established in 2006 as a new generation of AlMabrouk Group, which has been operating in oil and Gas Sector Since 1959 . in 2006 , Alfa Holding Group was Established with Diversified Activity in North Africa and Headquartered in Libya and its Subsidiaries Tunisia, Egypt and United Arab Emirates.
